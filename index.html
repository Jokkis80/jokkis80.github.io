Doomsday or a prosperous eternity: The AI in a nutshell

AI has been around for quite a while now and talked about in some cases – even movies depict AI either in a good or bad way. Now what is AI exactly?

AI – Artificial Intelligence

“Artificial Intelligence” describes one or multiple algorithms to solve a complex problem. Well, that is at least what I would describe it as. This is of course a broad topic which includes many different research fields, so it is difficult to come up with an appropriate definition. But AI is not just AI. There are three so called “calibers” of AI:

  • ANI – Artificial Narrow Intelligence
  • AGI – Artificial General Intelligence
  • ASI – Artificial Super Intelligence

ANI is the first of the AI’s and we, as humans, have already conquered this field (e.g. with a robot that can play chess or ping-pong better than a human). AGI is the next step and we are not yet able to make it, because AGI implies that an AI must be at least as Intelligent as a human being which is, at current times, quite a big challenge. ASI is a super Intelligence and as you can probably guess, it means that this AI is smarter than a human and just like a chimp would not understand a computer and how to use it, we could not even imagine what a super Intelligent AI would be able to do. But how could we even try to create something that is as smart as a human being? There are a few theories researchers came up with. One of these would be to scan a brain and try to copy it exactly into a machine but that proves to be difficult. Another way is to simulate evolution and create algorithms that “breed” with each other to create new and improved ones, these are called Genetic Algorithms.

Another important fact is that many experts believe that we are not far from creating AGI. So, we actually could already have a Computer with Human level of Intelligence in about 20 years. This never crossed my mind before and now hearing that it is this close makes me a bit anxious but also excited. I will be talking about these mixed feelings in the following text and explain why I’m feeling that way.

Excitement

There are many aspects which are great about the upcoming AI. Mostly because I myself could influence this field of research in a way and be part of this revolution. Another aspect is that ASI might be the last invention humankind will ever make and after that will live in a prosperous world. This AI could solve all problems we ever had and send us to immortality. This immortality concept is new, and no species has ever made it to immortality because at some point in time every species falls off the balance beam and goes right into extinction. Nanotechnology is also a big deal with the whole “escaping mortality” concept because it could be used to stop or even reverse aging in humans since aging is nothing more than physical things going on in our bodies. Ray Kurzweil is someone who has exactly these kinds of visions and I totally agree that this can happen. Another vision that we could use as a first step is that we just enhance ourselves with an orthosis which is driven by an AI, that way we don’t have a supercomputer just doing everything for us, but we make the computer a teammate that works only to improve us.

Anxiety

AI definitely has a side to it which can be at first very scary. If we take the example of “turry”, the future of humans could look very bad. Turry was at first a normal ANI that has been programmed to write a piece of paper and try to improve itself so it can write better and better. What the programmers didn’t know was that turry became soon an AGI and even sooner after that an ASI. So, it played dumb and after it got access to the internet it had all resources available to destroy humanity. Now why did it kill all humans? Turry probably thought that it would need more resources to improve and a way to ensure its persistence and the best way to do that was to stop all humans from taking resources by killing them. In this example we can see that if an AI is programmed the wrong way it could do a lot of harm and potentially be an existential risk. For it to work we would have to code all human values into the AI which can be difficult because not all values are the same for everyone.

Conclusive Words

This leaves us with the question: If an ASI is born, will it be a good God or will it turn evil in the sense that no one can control this machine anymore? And if an ASI is really smarter than us where is the cap on this or is there no intelligence cap? Or even if this super smart AI knows all we ever wanted to know but we wouldn’t be able to understand it, would it not be theoretically possible that the ASI has a solution to make every human super Intelligent too?

So finally, we can say that we don’t know what is going to happen with AI in the future, but we can surely predict certain aspects but still many questions will stay unanswered for some time.
